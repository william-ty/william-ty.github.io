I'm William Trahay, full stack software engineer with 6 years of experience building web and mobile products. I've led delivery end to end for startups, SMEs, web agencies and large enterprise clients.

My passion is solving problems and driving projects forward. I believe technology and tools serve the product, which is why I've worked across many environments and keep learning to pick the right stack for each challenge.

Among others, I've worked with TypeScript, React, React Native, Node.js and Nest.js APIs, AWS deployment, headless CMSs, C# and .NET, Microsoft Azure, PHP and Symfony, Docker, and many libraries along the way.

My skills range from project management and specs writing to scalable architecture design, code optimisation, DevOps and cloud practices, maintenance and support.

Contact me to talk about your project. I'd be very happy to accompany you on your journey and provide a quote.
